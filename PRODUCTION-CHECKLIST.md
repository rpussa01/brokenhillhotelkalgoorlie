# Production launch checklist

1. Confirm legal business name, ABN, GST treatment and receipt requirements.
2. Confirm menu, modifiers, allergens, prices and availability.
3. Configure Stripe in live mode and verify the webhook.
4. Replace JSON persistence with PostgreSQL.
5. Add email/SMS order confirmations.
6. Add printer/KDS alert sound and fallback order alert.
7. Test duplicate payment and webhook retry handling.
8. Test refunds, cancellations and sold-out changes.
9. Configure exact kitchen and pickup trading hours.
10. Add privacy, terms, refund and allergy disclaimers.
11. Test on iPhone, Android, tablets and the kitchen screen.
12. Run a controlled staff trial before public launch.
